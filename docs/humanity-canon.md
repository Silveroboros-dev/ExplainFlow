# Humanity Image Canon for ExplainFlow Background

This set is curated for **aesthetic quality**, **symbolic weight**, and **schematic clarity**.

## A. Human Condition (symbolic)
1. Vitruvian Man (Leonardo da Vinci)  
   - Why: human proportion, reason, design.  
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Vitruvian.jpg
2. Creation of Adam (Michelangelo)  
   - Why: consciousness, transmission of knowledge/energy.  
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:The_Creation_of_Adam_perspective_fix.jpg
3. Cueva de las Manos Handprints  
   - Why: first visual storytelling traces of humanity.  
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Paintings-Handprint-Cueva-de-las-Manos-Argentina.webp
4. Picasso Dove / Peace Dove motif  
   - Why: universal symbol of peace and human aspiration.  
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Peace_dove.svg

## B. Science & Schematics (ExplainFlow core language)
5. DNA Double Helix Schema  
   - Why: canonical life-information diagram.  
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:DNA_simple.svg
6. Feynman Diagram  
   - Why: compact visual reasoning in physics.  
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Feynman_Diagram_Gluon_Radiation.svg
7. Einstein E = mc² Formula  
   - Why: iconic bridge between abstraction and reality.  
   - Source: custom file in project (`web/public/humanity/einstein-equation.svg`).
8. Mandelbrot Set (fractal)  
   - Why: canonical visual of structured complexity and recursive pattern.
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Mandel_zoom_00_mandelbrot_set.jpg

## C. Civilization & Culture
9. Great Pyramid of Giza  
   - Why: ancient engineering and civilization scale.  
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Great_Pyramid_of_Giza.jpg
10. Chartres Cathedral  
   - Why: collective craft, geometry, and sacred architecture.  
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Cath%C3%A9drale_Notre-Dame_(Chartres)_(2).jpg
11. Sagrada Familia (Gaudí)  
   - Why: organic architecture and generative form.  
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Sagrada_Familia_03.jpg
12. Borobudur Buddha Statue (Java)  
   - Why: contemplative heritage and cultural memory.  
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Borobudur_-_Buddha_statue.jpg
13. Ahu Tongariki (Moai, Rapa Nui)  
   - Why: monumental human expression and ancestral continuity.  
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Ahu_Tongariki_cropped.jpg

## D. Planetary Perspective (shared context)
14. Earthrise (Apollo 8)  
   - Why: humanity seen as one species, one planet.  
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:NASA-Apollo8-Dec24-Earthrise.jpg
15. Blue Marble (Apollo 17)  
   - Why: complete visual symbol of shared home.  
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:The_Earth_seen_from_Apollo_17.jpg
