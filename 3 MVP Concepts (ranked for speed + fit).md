

1. **Explainer Director** (Best chance to ship fast)
    - What it is: Turn a topic/article/brief into an interleaved explainer stream: narration + diagrams/illustrations + captions + voiceover + scene timeline.
    - Why it fits: Native mixed output is obvious and demoable in 30 seconds.
    - MVP input: topic, audience, tone, duration.
    - MVP output: 6-scene storyboard with inline images + voiceover script + generated audio + social caption.
    - Risk: Low
    - Demo hook: “One prompt -> complete explainer package live.”

2. **Campaign Creative Director** (Best commercial vibe)
    - What it is: Marketing brief -> ad concept system with interleaved copy + key visuals + short video storyboard + platform variants.
    - Why it fits: Creative Storyteller + multimodal output + clear business use case.
    - MVP input: product brief + target audience + brand tone.
    - MVP output: hero concept, 3 visual directions, 15s storyboard, captions/hashtags.
    - Risk: Medium (visual consistency and polish matter)
    - Demo hook: “Launch-ready campaign kit generated in one flow.”

3. **Interactive Storybook Agent** (Highest wow, harder to finish)
- What it is: Story prompt -> illustrated story with narration audio, character art, scene cards, and branching choices.
- Why it fits: Very visible interleaving and creative category alignment.
- MVP input: characters + setting + lesson + age group.
- MVP output: 5 pages with inline art + narration + simple branch.
- Risk: Medium-high (UX and asset orchestration can eat time)
- Demo hook: “Live story generation with scenes, voices, and visuals.”

**My recommendation for you**

- Pick **#1 Explainer Director**.
- It uses your strength (turning messy input into coherent structure) without feeling like an enterprise governance product.
- It scores well on UX, architecture, and demo with less polish risk than #2/#3.
